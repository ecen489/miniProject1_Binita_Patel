Ingredients

1/2 cup mixed sliced vegetables, such as bell pepper,
	eggplant, and summer squash (see Tip)1 teaspoon olive oil
1 slice 100%-whole-wheat bread
2 tablespoons hummus
1/2 cup fresh spinach leaves
1 teaspoon unsalted raw sunflower seeds
2 fresh basil leaves, chopped

Step 1: Preheat oven to 350 F. Spread sliced vegetables on a small rimmed baking sheet and toss with olive oil.
	Roast until tender, about 25 minutes.

Step 2: Lightly toast bread, if desired. Spread hummus on the bread. Layer spinach on top, then top with the roasted 
	vegetables. Sprinkle with sunflower seeds and garnish with basil, if desired.

Tip: You can roast extra vegetables in Step 1; refrigerate, covered for up to 5 days. To reheat, microwave on High 
	for 30 seconds.
